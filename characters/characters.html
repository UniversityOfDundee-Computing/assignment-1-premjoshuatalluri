Characters

Gameplay

BARBARIAN

The Barbarian is a massive, heavily-armed warrior, a wanderer from a tribe that once guarded the sacred Mount Arreat.

  1. Fights savagely with melee weapons.
  2. Utilizes brute strength to wield mighty two-handed weapons, a weapon in both hands, or a weapon and shield.
  3. Builds up Fury when taking or dealing damage, then unleashes it in devastating attacks.

Skills

CRUSADER

The Crusader is a champion of justice adorned with metal armor and gleaming power. When evil emerges from its lair to twist and destroy humankind, the Crusader charges.

  1. Passes down judgment with cruel flails and towering shields—the latter as much weapon as armor.
  2. Forces the hosts of evil to fight (up-close or at medium range) with a myriad of abilities, and pronounces laws to augment the combat prowess of all who oppose darkness.
  3. Simmers with Wrath, a steadily mounting fervor, to unleash even more punishing attacks.

Skills

DEMON HUNTER

The Demon Hunter is the survivor of a demonic attack who has dedicated body and soul in an unending quest to rid the world of the influence of the demons that threaten Sanctuary.

  1. Deals death to foes with long-range weaponry like grenades, bows, and crossbows.
  2. Relies on Hatred, a swiftly regenerating inner fire, and Discipline, a moderate reservoir of marksmanship skill.
  3. Remains mobile with avoidance powers, and snares enemies in place with careful traps.

Skills

MONK

The Monk is a holy warrior, a servant of the divine whose body has been honed into a deadly weapon.

  1. Strikes down evil with lightning-quick fists and unique melee weapons like punch daggers and monk staves.
  2. Calls upon the power of his thousand and one gods to protect, shield, heal, and banish.
  3. Generates Spirit, an inner strength that fuels the monk’s mobility and dodging in combat.

Skills

NECROMANCER

The Necromancer harnesses terrifying powers of blood and bone to restore balance to Sanctuary, cursing his enemies with dreadful afflictions and commanding powerful skeletal armies and towering golems.

  1. Commands armies of skeletal soldiers and magi.
  2. Torments enemies with horrific curses, making them easy prey.
  3. Harvests Essence from enemies to gain mastery over life and death.

Skills

WITCH DOCTOR

The Witch Doctor weaves the power of the spirits of the Unformed Land, summoning creatures from beyond the grave.

  1. Summons frightful undead, vermin, and other creatures for protection and offense.
  2. Uses destructive poison, acid, and fire attacks.
  3. Powers spells and summons with Mana, a slow-accruing but deep well of energy.

Skills

WIZARD

The Wizard is an arcane prodigy who commands time and the elements in the pursuit of destiny and power.

  1. Wields arcane magics, manipulating space, time, and the natural and mystic elements.
  2. Freezes, incinerates, and restrains foes.
  3. Invokes magic rapidly with Arcane Power, a quickly-regenerating reservoir of power.
